# 1. Game Overview

## 1.1 Core Fantasy

> *"A lone cyber-farmer stands between a neon Ukrainian village and the endless horde of Soviet zombie bureaucrats."*

The player embodies **Serhiy** — a cyber-tractor driver wielding an energy cannon — defending the ancient Khutir (farmstead) of Lanchyn against **Mausoleum 2.1**, an informational anomaly that reanimates Soviet clerks, inspectors, and archivists as acid-neon zombies.

Core emotional pillars:
- **Defiance** — the small village holds against an overwhelming bureaucratic machine.
- **Absurdist Horror** — enemies are ridiculous yet genuinely threatening.
- **Folk Pride** — Ukrainian motifs are celebrated through neon aesthetics, not mocked.

## 1.2 Visual Identity

ACID KHUTIR uses the **Neon Psychedelic Cyber‑Folk** art direction:

- Heavy black outlines + cel-shading
- UV-reactive embroidery patterns on all Ukrainian elements
- Acid neon colour palette (Electric Blue, Neon Pink, Toxic Green, Ultra-Violet)
- Chromatic aberration and glitch effects on enemy corruption
- High contrast — every silhouette readable at a glance

## 1.3 Narrative

### Setting
The village of **Lanchyn**, somewhere between a folk tale and a cyberpunk nightmare. The year is unspecified.

### Inciting Event
The **Mausoleum 2.1** virus escapes a forgotten Soviet server farm buried under the village. It spreads through old paper documents, reanimating anyone who touches bureaucratic forms.

### Protagonist
**Serhiy** — retired tractor driver, folk musician, accidental hero. His tractor was retrofitted with an energy cannon by a mysterious travelling engineer.

### The Khata
The **Khutir** (home base) is a three-stage structure:
1. **Traditional** — thatched roof, sunflowers, wooden fence.
2. **Reinforced** — iron shutters, barbed wire, neon runes.
3. **Cyber-Sich** — plasma shields, holographic battlements, satellite dish.

### Boss
**Comrade Vakhtersha** — the immortal Soviet hall monitor, now fused with a massive metal desk. She fires rubber stamp artillery and deploys bureaucratic paper barriers.
